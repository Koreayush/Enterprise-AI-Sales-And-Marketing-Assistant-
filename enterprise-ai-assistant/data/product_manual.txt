Acme Enterprise Suite - Product Manual

Overview

Acme Enterprise Suite is a unified sales and marketing platform combining
CRM, email automation, and AI-powered customer insights into a single
dashboard.

Core Features

1. Contact Management: Centralized customer database with activity timelines,
   deal tracking, and custom fields.
2. Email Automation: Drip campaigns, A/B testing, and send-time optimization.
3. AI Insights: Predictive lead scoring and churn risk alerts powered by
   machine learning models trained on your historical deal data.
4. Reporting Dashboard: Real-time pipeline visibility with customizable
   reports and exportable charts.

Onboarding Process

New customers are assigned a dedicated implementation specialist who
guides setup over a typical 2-week onboarding period. This includes data
migration, integration configuration, and team training sessions.

Integrations

Acme integrates natively with Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Microsoft Teams,
Gmail, and Outlook. A public REST API and webhook support are available
on the Enterprise plan for custom integrations.

Support Tiers

- Professional plan: Standard support with a 24-hour response SLA via email.
- Enterprise plan: Priority support with a 2-hour response SLA, live chat,
  and a dedicated customer success manager.

Customer Success Stories

Mid-market and enterprise customers across healthcare, financial services,
and SaaS industries have used Acme to consolidate fragmented sales
tooling into a single platform, reducing administrative overhead for
sales teams.
